Our Story

Founded in 2020 by visionary entrepreneur Sanjar Tursunov, AZB Company emerged with a bold mission to revolutionize how businesses connect and thrive in the global marketplace. What started as an ambitious dream has grown into a trusted partner for companies across continents.

With over 5 years of combined industry experience, our team has consistently delivered exceptional results, building lasting relationships with clients spanning more than 10 countries. We believe in the power of innovation, dedication, and personalized service to transform businesses.

Today, AZB Company stands as a testament to what's possible when passion meets expertise, continuing to push boundaries and set new standards in our industry.
